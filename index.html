Hello! I'm Choi Beomgyu

Choi Beomgyu (최범규) is a South Korean singer, songwriter, and dancer. He was born on March 13, 2001, in Daegu, South Korea, and is known as a member of the boy group TOMORROW X TOGETHER (TXT) under BigHit Music. Beomgyu is known for his captivating visuals, musical talent, and cheerful personality. He also has guitar-playing skills and has written and co-produced several TXT songs.
